Spaghetti

Description:

This dish is spaghetti, an Italian classic you are all probably familiar with. In this recipe we are throwing a little spin on the dish, a variant of the dish called Spaghetti all'Assassina or in english The Assassin's Spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups tomato puree
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons red chili flakes, or to taste
  • 6 ounces dry spaghetti
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley, for garnish
